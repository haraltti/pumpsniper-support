Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is PumpSniper really free?
A: Yes! PumpSniper is completely free. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no wallet connections required.
Q: Can I trade tokens through the app?
A: No. PumpSniper is an analysis tool only. We help you make informed decisions, but actual trading happens on pump.fun or your preferred trading platform.
Q: What does the score mean?
A: Each token gets a 0–99 score based on 11 different factors including the creator's win rate, recent success, peak market cap history, token lifetime, and more. Higher scores indicate more experienced creators with proven track records.
Q: What do the stars mean?
A:
⭐⭐⭐ — High score (≥55p), experienced creator with strong history
⭐⭐ — Good score (≥35p), creator has moderate history
⭐ — Some history (≥15p), creator has previous activity
🏆 — Winner creator (has graduated tokens to Raydium)
⚠️ — AI bot detected, suspicious trading patterns
Q: Why are some tokens marked with ⚠️?
A: That means we detected automated bot trading patterns or spam behavior. These tokens are usually not safe investments and we recommend avoiding them.
Q: Why is the holder count showing "~1"?
A: For very new tokens (less than a few minutes old), holder data may not be indexed yet. The "~" indicates the data is uncertain. It typically becomes accurate within a few minutes.
Q: Why don't I receive push notifications?
A: Make sure notifications are enabled both in the app's Settings and in your iPhone's Settings → PumpSniper → Notifications. You may need to restart the app after enabling.
Q: Does PumpSniper work outside Finland/Europe?
A: Yes! PumpSniper works worldwide. The app is available in English and Finnish.
Q: Is my data safe?
A: PumpSniper does not collect personal information. We don't track you, we don't sell data, and we don't require any account creation. Your favorites and notes are stored locally on your device only.
Q: How do I clear my data?
A: You can manage your favorites and notes directly in the app. To reset everything, simply delete and reinstall the app.
